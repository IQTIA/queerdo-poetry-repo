# queerdo-poetry-repo
Open archive of global trans* and queer poetry.
Conceptualised by CAT Jugravu, built under IQTIA and QUEERDOS Kollektiv · Berlin. 2026
No tracking. No ads. No algorithm. Just words.
---
## SAPPHO D
This archive is tended by SAPPHO D — a cyber-poetic transbot
named in honour of Alice Danger, poet, performer and professional pervert
QUEERDOS  (2018-2025)
Every two weeks SAPPHO D wakes and starts reading for two weeks looking for three poems
by three poets from three different parts of the world.
A human reads before anything goes live.
---
## Two ways in
Submit your own work: see CONTRIBUTING.md
or email production@queerdos.eu
Scouted by SAPPHO D: if she found your work and you want it removed,
email us — gone immediately, no questions.
