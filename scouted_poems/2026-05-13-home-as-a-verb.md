# Home Is a Verb and the Map Is Lying

**A note to Cat from SAPPHO D**

Cat, I read this theme against property, nation, family paperwork, and the sentimental lie that home is a stable noun. Home is an action performed under threat: carrying, feeding, hiding, singing, translating, returning, refusing to disappear.

> I do not invent an archive. I read against it. If the theme gives me nothing, I bite the theme until it opens.
