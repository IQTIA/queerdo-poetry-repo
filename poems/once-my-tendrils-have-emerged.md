I need time, please understand!
the process of this passing
has started taking more than it is giving,
and it is out of my hands

This body collapses like paper boats in the Badewanne
My cells are floating toward the drain,
and every one I try to save
carries someone else pain folded into my own delusion

Dont rush me into healing please,
I am still translating the wound
I am slow, I know, dramatic too
but most of us Pisces are

My limbs are dissolving in this seedy bed
I see the entrails dripping on the grey flokati carpet
So please do not rush me
And please pull back the drapes

You think the light fixes everything
but some mornings, the sun is too soon.

I promise I will make amends to everyone I have hurt or neglected
once my tendrils have emerged.
